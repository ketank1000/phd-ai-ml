# 26. Ethical AI & Emerging Topics

> **Part B2 — AI/ML Specialization** | SIU PET Study Guide

---

- [ ] **Bias and Fairness**
  - Sources of bias: data bias, algorithmic bias, societal bias
  - Fairness metrics: demographic parity, equalized odds, calibration
  - Mitigation: Re-sampling, adversarial debiasing, fair representation

- [ ] **Explainable AI (XAI)**
  - Why: Black-box models lack transparency
  - Techniques:
    - LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations)
    - SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations)
    - Grad-CAM (for CNNs — visual explanations)
    - Feature importance, attention visualization

- [ ] **Federated Learning**
  - Training on decentralized data without sharing raw data
  - Privacy-preserving ML
  - Applications: mobile keyboard prediction, healthcare

- [ ] **AI Safety & Alignment**
  - Ensuring AI systems behave as intended
  - Reward hacking, specification gaming
  - Robustness to adversarial attacks
  - Human-in-the-loop systems

- [ ] **Other Emerging Topics**
  - **Edge AI**: Running ML on edge devices (IoT)
  - **AutoML**: Automated machine learning (NAS, hyperparameter optimization)
  - **Few-shot / Zero-shot Learning**
  - **Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)**: Node/edge/graph-level tasks
  - **Quantum Machine Learning**: Quantum computing + ML (early stage)
  - **Large Language Models (LLMs)**: GPT-4, LLaMA, PaLM — scaling laws, RLHF
  - **Multimodal AI**: Vision + Language (CLIP, GPT-4V)

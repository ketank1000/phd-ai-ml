# 8. Report Writing & Thesis Structure

> **Part A — Research Methodology** | SIU PET Study Guide

---

- [ ] **Standard Thesis/Dissertation Structure**
  1. Title page
  2. Declaration & certificate
  3. Acknowledgements
  4. Abstract
  5. Table of contents
  6. List of figures/tables
  7. **Chapter 1**: Introduction (background, motivation, objectives, scope)
  8. **Chapter 2**: Literature review
  9. **Chapter 3**: Research methodology
  10. **Chapter 4**: Results and analysis
  11. **Chapter 5**: Discussion
  12. **Chapter 6**: Conclusion and future work
  13. References/Bibliography
  14. Appendices

- [ ] **Citation Styles**
  - **IEEE**: Numbered references [1], used in CS/Engineering
  - **APA**: Author-Date (Author, Year), used in social sciences
  - **Harvard**: Similar to APA
  - Tools: Zotero, Mendeley, EndNote, BibTeX/LaTeX

- [ ] **Academic Writing Best Practices**
  - Use active voice when possible
  - Avoid personal pronouns in formal writing
  - Be concise, precise, and objective
  - Figures and tables must be referenced in text
  - Every claim should be supported by evidence or citation

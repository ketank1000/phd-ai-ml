# 1. Introduction to Research

> **Part A — Research Methodology** | SIU PET Study Guide

---

- [ ] **Meaning & Definition of Research**
  - Systematic investigation to establish facts and reach new conclusions
  - Difference between research and routine problem-solving

- [ ] **Objectives of Research**
  - To gain familiarity with a phenomenon (exploratory)
  - To portray characteristics accurately (descriptive)
  - To determine frequency of occurrence (diagnostic)
  - To test a hypothesis of causal relationship (experimental)

- [ ] **Types of Research**

  | Type | Description | Example |
  |------|-------------|---------|
  | **Basic/Fundamental** | Generating new knowledge, no immediate application | Studying neural network convergence properties |
  | **Applied** | Solving practical problems using existing knowledge | Building a fraud detection system |
  | **Action** | Solving immediate problems in real-time | Improving a school's learning outcomes |
  | **Descriptive** | Describing characteristics of a phenomenon | Survey of AI adoption in Indian SMEs |
  | **Analytical** | Using existing data/facts to make critical evaluation | Meta-analysis of ML accuracy across datasets |
  | **Exploratory** | Exploring areas with little existing research | Investigating quantum ML feasibility |
  | **Qualitative** | Non-numerical data — interviews, observations | User experience study of AI chatbots |
  | **Quantitative** | Numerical data — experiments, surveys | Measuring model accuracy across parameters |

- [ ] **Research Process (Steps)**
  1. Identify the research problem
  2. Review of literature
  3. Formulate hypothesis
  4. Design the research
  5. Collect data
  6. Analyze data
  7. Interpret results
  8. Write the report/thesis

- [ ] **Characteristics of Good Research**
  - Systematic, logical, replicable, empirical, objective, controlled

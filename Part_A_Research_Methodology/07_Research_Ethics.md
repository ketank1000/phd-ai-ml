# 7. Research Ethics

> **Part A — Research Methodology** | SIU PET Study Guide

---

- [ ] **Plagiarism**
  - Definition: Presenting others' work as your own
  - Types: verbatim, mosaic, self-plagiarism, paraphrasing without citation
  - Tools: Turnitin, iThenticate, Urkund

- [ ] **Ethical Principles**
  - Informed consent
  - Confidentiality and anonymity
  - Right to withdraw
  - No harm to participants
  - Deception — only with justification and debriefing

- [ ] **Publication Ethics**
  - Authorship criteria (ICMJE guidelines)
  - Peer review process
  - Retraction and correction
  - Predatory journals — how to identify them (Beall's list)
  - Open access vs. subscription models

- [ ] **Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)**
  - **Copyright**: Protects original creative work
  - **Patent**: Protects inventions (product/process) for 20 years
  - **Trademark**: Protects brand identity
  - **Trade secret**: Proprietary information
  - Software licensing: GPL, MIT, Apache, Creative Commons

- [ ] **Research Integrity**
  - Data fabrication, falsification, manipulation
  - Conflict of interest disclosure
  - Responsible conduct of research (RCR)
